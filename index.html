The guy that makes me keep going on

Joseph Edward Mulheirn better known by his stage name nothing, nowhere is an American singer, rapper and songwriter. His songs vary between emo rap, trap, indie rock, and punk pop.

Here is a time line of n,n life:

  • 4/6/1992 - Born in Massachusetts, USA
  • 2015 - Started uploading songs on SoundCloud under the name nothing,nowhere and released the album titled the nothing, nowhere. LP onto Bandcamp
  • 20/10/2017 - n,n released his commercial debut, Reaper, an album of guitar-based emo rap that the New York Times called "one of the most promising pop albums of the year"
  • 2017 - New York Times music critic Jon Caramanica listed Reaper as his #1 album of 2017
  • 16/2/2018 - It was confirmed that Mulherin had signed to Fueled by Ramen as he released a new single titled "Ruiner"
  • 13/4/2018 - Released a new album titled like his last single "Ruiner" which contains the song.
  • 2018 - Mulherin was on tour in Europe
  • 1/28/2020 - Released a new single titled "Nightmare" and announces dates for a world tour, but these dates were soon cancelled in march of the same year due to complications involving the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • 2020 - Released various singles through the year like "Death", "Lights (4444)", "Pretend" and "Blood". Also released an album titled "One Takes: Vol. 1" that features versions of songs released prior that were all recorded in one take.
  • 7/12/2020 - Mulherin announced his upcoming fourth album, "Trauma Factory"
  • 19/2/2021 - Released the album "Trauma Factory" and subsequently released a new single for the album, titled "Fake Friend"
